
# 📊 End-to-End Telecom Customer Churn Analysis

An end-to-end Telecom Customer Churn Analysis project built using **SQL Server, Power BI, Excel, and DAX** to identify churn drivers, customer segments, revenue impact, and retention opportunities through interactive dashboards.

---

# 📌 Business Problem

Customer churn directly impacts revenue and business growth. Telecom companies need to identify customers who are likely to leave and understand the factors driving churn.

This project analyzes customer demographics, subscription details, billing behavior, service usage, and customer support interactions to uncover actionable business insights.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

- Identify customers who are most likely to churn.
- Analyze the major drivers behind customer churn.
- Measure churn rate and revenue impact.
- Segment customers based on contract type, payment method, and services.
- Build interactive dashboards for business decision-making.
- Provide data-driven retention recommendations.

# 🔍 Key Business Insights

- Customers using **Fiber Optic Internet** have the highest churn rate.
- **Electronic Check** users are more likely to churn.
- Gender has minimal impact on churn.
- Customers with **Partners** and **Dependents** are more loyal.
- Long-term contracts significantly reduce churn.
- Customers using **Online Security**, **Online Backup**, **Device Protection**, and **Tech Support** have lower churn.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

### Improve Fiber Internet Service

- Improve network quality.
- Reduce downtime.
- Offer loyalty discounts.

### Promote Auto-Pay

- Encourage Credit Card and Bank Transfer payments.
- Offer cashback and discounts.

### Increase Long-Term Contracts

- Promote annual and two-year plans.
- Offer bundled pricing.

### Increase Service Adoption

Promote:

- Online Security
- Online Backup
- Device Protection
- Tech Support

through free trials and bundled offers.

### Improve Customer Support

- Train support agents.
- Recommend retention services during customer interactions.

# 📌 Conclusion

Approximately **26.5%** of customers have churned.

The strongest churn drivers are:

- Fiber Optic Internet
- Electronic Check Payment
- Month-to-Month Contracts

By improving service quality, promoting auto-pay methods, increasing adoption of value-added services, and encouraging long-term contracts, telecom companies can significantly improve customer retention and long-term profitability.
